Lorry Driver to Coder

4th October 2020 at 12:45

Back in May 2011…
For several personal reasons, I trained to become an HGV Lorry Driver. Having completed the test I was able to hit the road with one of the UK largest hauliers.

Within a few weeks, I was working as a tramper. Staying away from home, living in the cab during the week. Long hours averaging about 13hrs a day. Being out in all weather conditions.

Moving onto the summer of 2020…

I was still enjoying the driving side of the job, although other aspects have finally worn me down. I was thinking of a career change but could not think of what to do.

One day I received a phone call from the Code Institute which would change my life.

Over the next few blog posts, my story will continue……..
